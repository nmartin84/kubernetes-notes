Workloads in Kubernetes are objects that define _how applications run on Pods_. They describe the desired state of your app (e.g., how many replicas, update strategy) and let Kubernetes manage the underlying Pods automatically.

> [!NOTE] Workload Types
> - [CronJob](cronjob.md)
> - [Job](job.md)
> - [DaemonSet](daemonset.md)
> - [StatefulSet](statefulset.md)
> - [ReplicaSet](replicaset.md)
> - [Deployment](deployment.md)
